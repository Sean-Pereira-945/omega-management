Design a bold, playful inventory products dashboard for Omega Management. It should feel like a premium ops console with warm neutrals, large rounded cards, and a sun-washed gradient background.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Theme: Light, warm neutrals with ink-black anchors
- Background: Dune Cream (#f7f2ea) with subtle radial gradients
- Primary Text: Deep Ink Black (#0d0f1a)
- Secondary Text: Ink Slate (#1e2233)
- Accent 1: Sun Flame (#ff7a45) for alerts and filters
- Accent 2: Mint Seafoam (#3fcaa4) for success states
- Surfaces: Dune Sand (#efe7da) cards with Dune Clay (#e2d6c3) borders
- Typography: Space Grotesk for headings, DM Sans for body
- Corners: 24-28px rounded cards, pill buttons, rounded inputs
- Shadows: Soft diffused shadows under cards and dropdowns

**Page Structure:**
1. **Left Sidebar:** Logo, main nav (Products, Analytics), and compact user profile card.
2. **Top Bar:** Breadcrumbs, page title, quick actions, and a right-aligned profile chip.
3. **Filter Row:** Search input, category multi-select pills, sort dropdown, and view toggle.
4. **KPI Rail:** 3-4 highlight cards (Total Products, Avg Rating, Inventory Value, Low Stock).
5. **Products Table:** Rich table with thumbnail, name, category, price, rating, stock, status badges, and inline action buttons.
6. **Secondary Panel:** A mini chart or category breakdown card, aligned to the right on desktop.
7. **Footer Strip:** Subtle status line with sync time and system health badge.

**Interaction Notes:**
- Use pill-shaped buttons and uppercase micro-labels.
- Keep table rows airy with soft separators and hover highlight.
- Include a small live-update badge near the table header.
