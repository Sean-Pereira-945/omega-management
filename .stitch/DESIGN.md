# Design System: Omega Management Dashboard

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Bright, optimistic operations dashboard with soft, sun-washed surfaces and warm neutrals. The UI feels modern, lively, and slightly tactile, with generous padding, rounded corners, and clear visual hierarchy.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- Deep Ink Black (#0d0f1a) for primary headings and nav emphasis.
- Ink Slate (#1e2233) for body text and strong labels.
- Ink Smoke (#3b4158) for secondary labels.
- Dune Cream (#f7f2ea) for page background and panel surrounds.
- Dune Sand (#efe7da) for card and table surfaces.
- Dune Clay (#e2d6c3) for borders and separators.
- Sun Flame (#ff7a45) for alerts and highlight badges.
- Sun Ember (#ea5d2c) for stronger alert emphasis.
- Mint Seafoam (#3fcaa4) for success and availability accents.

## 3. Typography Rules
- Display: Space Grotesk, semi-bold for titles and key numbers.
- Body: DM Sans, regular for paragraphs and labels.
- Uppercase micro-labels with wide letter spacing for sections.

## 4. Component Stylings
- Buttons: pill-shaped, bold uppercase micro-labels, subtle shadow on primary.
- Cards/Containers: large rounded corners (24-28px), soft diffused shadow.
- Inputs/Filters: rounded-2xl, pale background, thin dune borders.
- Tables: rounded header corners, subtle zebra separators, inline status badges.

## 5. Layout Principles
- 12-column desktop grid with left sidebar and flexible content.
- Generous whitespace between panels, with dense content only inside tables.
- Keep the filter row elevated as the primary interaction zone.
