# Omega Management Site

## 1. Vision
A playful but professional inventory dashboard with bold typography, soft gradients, and data-rich surfaces. The experience should feel fast, modern, and easy to scan.

## 2. Audience
Operations teams and product managers monitoring inventory, pricing, and performance in real time.

## 3. Design Direction
Warm neutrals, ink-black anchors, and bright accent badges. Layouts emphasize rapid scanning and quick actions.
